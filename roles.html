Roles

On this page we'll detail what each roles is and what they do.
The roles are as follows:

  • Director
  • Staff
  • Writers
  • Coders
  • Artists & Animators
  • Voice Actors
  • Singers
  • Snippet Writers
  • Editors
  • Puzzlemakers
  • Musicians
  • Lyricists

Director

Our director, the driving force of the project, Fireboa. He's the founder of the project and is one of the main workers on this project helping bring this project to life.

Staff

Our staff, the people who moderate the server and keep it running in tip top shape.

Writers

The Writers, the ones who put the soul into the project, bringing it from some ideas on a page to a fleshed out story.

Voice Actors

The Voice Actors, the voices of the project, adding that layer of immerision to pull you into their world.

The Musicians play & write music, to which the Singers sing the songs to, which the Lyricists have written. All coming together to make a lovely cacophany of sounds for the project.

Snippet Writers

Snippet Writers are the writers who make all the newspaper articles, diary entries, small scripts and so much more, they help add a little more depth to the world.

Editors

Our editing team, works with everyone else to assemble the final bits to be put up.

Oh the puzzles, a crucial part of any ARG, hiding all the little details behind layers of ciphers and obscurity.
